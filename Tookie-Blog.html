A Day In the Life of Tookie

Tookie's day starts by waking her mom up at 6:30am for breakfast. After snarfing down every last bit of food, Tookie enjoys a brief siesta for around 3 hours. When she is well digested and well rested, it's time for a bit of exploration. She nags and nags until her mom agrees to take her for a walk around the neighborhood. Upon her return it is time for her second siesta... another 3 hours down! After that she passes the time by barking at any noise, in particular that of a UPS truck or mail-person. Dinner time! More scarfing, sometimes some barfing, what a day it has been!
